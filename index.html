⊙ FAQ's

Answers

Find answers to common questions about our AI process, deployment timelines, and engagement models.

🔬
What AI services do you provide?
+
We specialize in enterprise AI strategy, custom model development (LLMs, computer vision, NLP), MLOps infrastructure, and deep integration with existing enterprise systems including ERP, CRM, and cloud platforms.
How do I start working with NexusAI?
+
Book a free discovery call through our website. We'll scope your requirements, assess your data readiness, and present a tailored proposal within 5 business days.
How long does a typical project take?
+
MVP deployments typically take 6–12 weeks. Full-scale enterprise platforms range from 3–6 months. We use agile sprints so you see working software every 2 weeks.
How do you handle data security and compliance?
+
We operate under enterprise-grade NDAs, SOC 2 compliant infrastructure, and support GDPR, HIPAA, and SOX compliance frameworks. Your data never leaves your approved environment.
Do you offer ongoing support after deployment?
+
Yes. We offer managed MLOps retainers that include model monitoring, retraining, performance reporting, and dedicated engineering support — so your AI systems improve over time.
What industries do you serve?
+
Healthcare, financial services, logistics, manufacturing, legal, and professional services. Any industry with complex data workflows and high manual processing overhead is a strong fit.
What is your pricing model?
+
We offer fixed-scope project engagements, time-and-materials retainers, and outcome-based pricing for well-defined automation use cases. We'll recommend the model that best fits your objectives.
